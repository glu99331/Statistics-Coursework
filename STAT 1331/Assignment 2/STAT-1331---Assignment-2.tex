\subsection{Problem 1:}\label{problem-1}}

Let \{\(x_t\) for t = \ldots, -2, -1, 0, 1, 2, \ldots\} be a time series
process. 1a) What does it mean when we say \(x_t\) is weakly stationary?

\hypertarget{solution}{%
\subsubsection{Solution:}\label{solution}}

constant mean and variance, and the covariance between \(x_{t1}\) and
